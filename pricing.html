FAQ

Questions?

What happens after the 14-day trial?

Full functionality during the trial — no lockouts, no nags. From day 10 onward, a gentle daily reminder. On day 14 a pop-up invites you to buy a license. Unpaid sessions can still run without mappings firing.

Can I transfer a license to another Mac?

Yes. Deactivate the license on one Mac in Settings, then activate it on another. Each license runs on the number of Macs its tier allows, concurrently.

Is there a subscription?

No. Every tier is a one-time purchase with every future update included. Pay once, use forever.

Do I need an internet connection?

Only to activate your license key the first time and to check for updates. DoubleTap runs fine offline — up to 30 days between verifications.

What macOS versions are supported?

macOS 13 (Ventura) and later, optimized for macOS 26 Tahoe. Universal binary: Apple Silicon and Intel both.

Is my data safe?

Yes. No tracking of any kind. Your shortcuts are stored only on your Mac. DoubleTap only reaches out to the internet to verify your license and check for updates.
