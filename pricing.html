Questions?

What happens after the trial ends?

DoubleTap keeps working but shows a reminder to purchase. No features are removed during the trial — you get the full experience from day one.

Can I transfer a license to another Mac?

Yes. Deactivate on one Mac and activate on another. Each license key works on one Mac at a time.

Is there a subscription?

No. Every plan is a one-time purchase with lifetime updates included. Pay once, use forever.

Do I need an internet connection?

Only once — to activate your license key. After that, DoubleTap runs 100% offline.

What macOS versions are supported?
